About Panacea

Panacea is a disease tracking application that uses the power of crowdsourcing to track communicable disease outbreaks in real-time. Users can look at an aggregated map of all current global disease outbreaks or search for specific diseases by name and location.

Panacea tracks communicable disease outbreak as they happen. The goal is to develop a crowdsourcing alert system that not only promotes more awareness about various disease outbreaks, but also creates a better response system in certain poor and developing countries where disease tracking can be a challenge for health agencies.

Data is sourced from Twitter, Web Forms, and SMS. Users can submit reports of diseases via the web form or SMS.
